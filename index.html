Peace Tech Journey

An enthusiatic young lady.

The journey towards a great future.

Started out with the love of tech by delving into Computer Science in a Nigerian Institution yet came out in 2015 with no practical knowledge of programming due to the nature of what the school entails. Started with freecodecame & codeacademy after higher institution but lost interest on the way.

Got married in 2016 then went into a one month internship as a social media marketer in 2017. Then another one month code camp on becoming a Full Stack Developer early in 2018, then finished her NYSC in Dec of that year. She was really set on fire but with no mentor,the fire died easily since it wasn't easy to be self motivated on this path & perform wifely/motherly duties.

After some time, Peace started some online courses trying to build herself up while the motivation is back in 2019. Used some youtube videos, goes back & forth on freecodecame, sololearn, udemy etc. She then went for another 3 months internship which ended Dec, 2019.

This internship was what gave her the actual direction & a living fire that she needed. January, 2020, pave for her in her first mentorship program, on becoming a real software enginner, which she combined with getting to understanding the world of UI/UX. It has been mind blowing although overwhelming sometimes but fortunately, it has dawn on her that every pain & struggle will be worth it.
